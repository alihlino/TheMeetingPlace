Chapter 6: Long Lasting Love

Scene 4: Forever and Always

The girl is crying her heart out with all the realization that she can no longer be calm. Many people noticed her and they are murmuring and asking each other if the guy is talking about her. "It is not the distance that will keep us separated. It is our feeling that will. I have an offer to take a job by the end of this month. But, whatever happens, always remember that I will love you. If you still do, please text me." The audience laughed at this point. "Kidding aside, the commencement exercise ends our college life and begins our new and real life. Whoever you are. I want to start my NEW and REAL life with you. Let's start over."
