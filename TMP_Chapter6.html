Chapter 6: Long Lasting Love

Scene 1: Lovely Girl from Behind

Months had passed and everything's seems back to normal for both of them. It is as if it nothing happened.
The girl, being very beautiful and like-able, continued to reject all guys that was trying to court her.
Whenever she opened her campus locker, different gifts showed up-- some are very weird stuff.
From big things like a branded New York best-selling books and to small things like Mediterranean styled handkerchief,
an Eiffel tower keychain and a pair of Venetian earrings,
to sweet things like Belgian dark chocolate and beautiful bouquet of tropical flowers.
Of course, none of that stuff meant anything to her.
She wants none of those things nor the guys who secretly been putting those in front of her locker.
