Chapter 2: The day he lied

Scene 1: The Decision

Craig was one of the handsome guys in the university. He was so depressed because of what happened between him and his girlfriend.
For him to totally move on and forget his past, he decided to go to Manila and stay there for good to study in a University wherein
he met this beautiful girl named Martha. Love at first sight? People say that is not true.
Though Craig doesn't believe in those sayings, since he met this girl, he thinks that love at first sight was true.

Without further thinking, with his heart still broken, full of fear about rejection, Craig find ways to get the cell phone number of Martha.
"Hi, I wonder if you have the cell phone number of Martha?
I'm one of her friends and I really need to talk to her since she wasn't in the university on Tuesdays."
He said to one of her classmate. Hours passed by and he still not got what he wants. Since Martha is one of the heartthrobs in the university,
her personal life and social life was so confidential. She doesn't let anyone know of her number unless they were really close.

"I gave-up easily last time, so I lost her." He said to himself. Determination of him comes out just not to lose a special girl again.
