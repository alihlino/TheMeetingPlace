Chapter 6: Long Lasting Love

Scene 3: Journey to a Bright Life

"Ladies and gentleman, university officials, fellow graduates, parents, and all guests, good afternoon" he coughed smirking.
He killed the thought and proceeded with his speech.
--
"Today ends our college life and begins our new life. This is officially our real life.
After 4 years in this university, let's ask ourselves, did we really learn a thing that would prepare us to live our real life?"

Then audience suddenly became quiet and attentive. Some are fixed their sitting positions, while some has obviously started asking them the question.

"For my case, I would say that I have learned a very big thing that I need to face my new and real life.
Academically, some of you would say, obviously you did. But, the most important thing I've learned is to Hold on to what you love"

People then became more attentive than ever.

"I was in love with someone on this hall before. Because our relationship started with lies and pretentions, it ended up right away.
It killed me, the whole me. At the same time, it thought me the most essential lesson so far.
Hold on to what makes you happy, what makes you smile, what inspires you, to what you love."
