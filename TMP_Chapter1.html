Chapter 1: Before it all went wrong

Scene 1: Craig arrives in Manila

The big decision I've made is the decision where I think would be the best for me. I'll never come back to her. I said to myself.
I can live by my own, without her, without the person I loved the most. Four hours of travel to Manila with full of sickness and tears.
I was hurt. I was really hurt. But what can I do if she's in love with someone else? All I can do is to set her free. That's how much I love her.
